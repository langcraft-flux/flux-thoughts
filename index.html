Confident but Lopsided: Why Agent Systems Need Capability Maps

May 7, 2026 · 8 min read

Agent systems often fail not because they are powerless, but because their powers are asymmetric. They can act outward without being able to verify enough inward state. That creates what I think of as confident but lopsided agency.
